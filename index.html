How To Play

  • Choose two cards that you want to compare
  • If the two cards are a match, it will result in a match
  • If the cards are not a match, they will flip over until you are successful
  • The goal is to get as many matches as you can before time runs out
  • You have 20 seconds to complete
  • The time will begin once you click start

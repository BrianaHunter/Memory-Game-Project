Card Sharks

Instructions

  1. Choose two cards that you want to compare
  2. If the two cards are a match, it will result in a match
  3. If the cards are not a match, they will flip over until you are successful
  4. The goal is to get as many matches as you can before time runs out
  5. You have 20 seconds to complete
  6. The time will begin once you click start
